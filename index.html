Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score, shown in the pink box below.


The value of each crystal is randomly determined each game, and will be hidden from you until you click on it.


You win the game by matching your total score to the number displayed in the green box below. If your score exceeds this number, you lose.
